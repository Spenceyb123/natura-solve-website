Comes with instructions for use and enough product for approx. 300-375 acres  of property. Mix as directed for specific use. 

USES

SoilMix has a number of agricultural and domestic uses, including lawn care, composting, fungus control, water need reduction, fertilizer need reduction, and agricultural yield increase.
SoilMix is safe to use around children, pets, and bees.

DOSING

General Use: Add 1 oz to 5 gallons of water. Apply to desired area with spray applicator. Each 5 gallon mix should cover ~⅙ acre. Total SoilMix use should be ~6.4 oz of SoilMix per acre. In other words, 1 gallon of SoilMix should be able to treat 20-25 acres once, 10-13 acres twice, 7-8 acres three times, etc.

Apply every 2 weeks for 6 weeks.

Maintenance: Add 1 oz to 5 gallons of water. Apply to desired area with spray applicator. This should cover ~⅓ acre. Total use should be ~3.2 oz of SoilMix per acre.

Apply every 2 weeks.

Composting: In addition to spraying your lawn and/or fields, spray your compost pile as well. For independent application to compost piles, dip toothpick in SoilMix and stir collected SoilMix into 4 cups of water. Add to compost pile. For best results, use spray applicator.

For Agriculture Use: Adequate
application of SoilMix reduces the amount of fertilizer needed to
maintain healthy plants. After first application, reduce fertilizer used
by ~25%. Over the course of 4 weeks, reduce your total
fertilizer use by ~50%.

Water Use: Adequate application of SoilMix reduces the amount of water needed to maintain healthy plants. After the first application, over the course of 5 weeks, gradually reduce water use by up to 10% per week. Expect a 30-50% reduction in total water needed to maintain healthy plants.
