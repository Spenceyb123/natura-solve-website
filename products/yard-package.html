USES

SoilMix has a number of agricultural and domestic uses, including lawn care, composting, fungus control, water need reduction, fertilizer need
reduction, and agricultural yield increase.
SoilMix is safe to use around children, pets, and bees.

DOSING

General Use: Add 1 oz to 5 gallons of water. Apply to desired area with spray applicator. Each 5 gallon mix should cover ~⅙ acre. Total SoilMix use should be ~6.4 oz of SoilMix per acre. In other words, 1 gallon of SoilMix should be able to treat 20-25 acres once, 10-13 acres twice, 7-8 acres three times, etc.Apply every 2 weeks for 6 weeks.

Apply every 2 weeks for 6 weeks.

Maintenance: Add 1 oz to 5 gallons of water. Apply to desired area with spray applicator. This should cover ~⅓ acre. Total use should be ~3.2 oz of SoilMix per acre.

Apply every 2 weeks.

Composting: In addition to spraying your lawn and/or fields, spray your compost pile as well. For independent application to compost piles, dip toothpick in SoilMix and stir collected SoilMix into 4 cups of water. Add to compost pile. For best results, use spray applicator.

For Agriculture Use: Adequate application of SoilMix reduces the amount of fertilizer needed to maintain healthy plants. After first application, reduce fertilizer used by ~25%. Over the course of 4 weeks, reduce your total fertilizer use by ~50%.

Water Use: Adequate application of SoilMix reduces the amount of water needed to maintain healthy plants. After the first application, over the course of 5 weeks, gradually reduce water use by up to 10% per week. Expect a 30-50% reduction in total water needed to maintain healthy plants.

Usage Intstructions:

Fill tank with appropriate amount of granular or liquid chemical. Always consult chemical manufacturer’s instructions before use. When using granular products, be sure that the granules are contained  in the ﬁlter basket. Liquid concentrates should be poured thru the ﬁlter basket when ﬁlling tank.

Firmly tighten tank onto mixing head. Do not over tighten. Adjust yellow dial to set desired amount of concentrate to be mixed with water. Follow manufacturer’s instructions for mix ratios (Ounces: OZ, Tablespoons: TBS, Teaspoons: TSP). Choose spray pattern by rotating nozzle selector to desired output (SHOWER, STREAM, DEFLECTOR UP, DEFLECTOR DOWN). To activate sprayer, use thumb dial on handle. Choose WET setting for Liquid Concentrates or DRY Setting for granular mixes.

WARNING:
 Always wear eye protection. Do not spray during windy conditions. Clean  sprayer thoroughly after each use. Do not substitute nozzles. Do not  set dials between numbers. Before using read and understand chemical manufacturer’s instructions and warnings as well as those in sprayer  instruction sheet.
