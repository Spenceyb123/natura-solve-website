USES

WaterMix can be used to resolve a number of aquatic issues from algae control to heavy metal chelation. Depending on your application, follow the dosing guidelines found below. 
DO NOT USE WITH CHLORINATED WATERIF DISSOLVED OXYGEN IS BELOW 2%, AERATE WATER

DOSING

General Use: For heavy algae, light to moderate hydrocarbon contamination, and mosquito control, add 1 oz per 1,500 gallons every two weeks for 6 weeks, then reduce to Light Dosing

Light Dosing: To maintain water clarity, add 1 oz per 5,000 gallons every 3 weeks.

Waste Water: For waste water streams, heavy fat/hydrocarbon contamination, add 1 oz per 300-500 gallons every 2 weeks.

Mine Tailings & Pregnant Stations: For mine tailings and other industrial heavy metal applications, add 1 oz per 1000 gallons every 2 weeks. 

Sludge Tank Treatment: 1 oz per 300-500 gallons for one treatment, then 1 oz per 1,500 gallons for 2 treatments and 1 oz per 5,000 for maintenance. 

Frac Water Treatment: 1 oz per 600-1,200 gallons for 2 treatments and 1 oz per 1,500 ongoing or until treatment is complete. 

Hydrocarbon Rinsing Agent: 1 oz per 1,500-5,000 (depending on density of hydrocarbons) per gallons of aerated, polluted waters or mix 5 oz in a 5 gallon sprayer and spray surface of waters.
