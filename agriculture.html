OUR LIQUID

Why try our products?

  • Low to no cost -  Cut fertilizers by 30-40% right away, giving you space for the product’s minimal cost.
  • Cut your water use - By 30-52% on average. Your soil will be conditioned and healthy.
  • Increased Yield -Less resources and a bigger crop, due to increased plant health and flowering.

How do I start?

Pick an Area

Choose an acre or more and try our one gallon, or get the 5 gal bottle (if you
have a larger operation) and test a few crops and soil sites. Have your space marked to  compare to your non-treated area.

Mix with water at recommended levels and spray on soil, around or on crops and on green waste, for added benefit. 

Spray after regular fertilization and/or just after you water through hand sprayers, truck mounted sprayers, pressurized irrigation, or your  sprinkler system.

Example Recommended Dosage:  Mix with water at about 1200:1 gallons water to product ounces. ~6.4 oz of total SoilMix per acre (to begin, you'll pull this back as we go). 

Lets say we're using a 5 gallon backpack sprayer - we'll add 5 gallons water
(640 oz) and 1 oz product and apply. This would cover about 1/6 and
acre before you'd refill.

You can mix in a hand sprayer, backpack sprayer or in irrigation ponds for ideal use. For ponds and pressurized irrigation, pour water and product (at the same 1oz per every 1,200 gallons of water in your pond) into 5 gallon bucket, mix and pour into pond or holding area.

We typically see use at about 1 gallon used per 20-25 acres (depending on soil, crop type and climate). To note - No, it won't "gum things up" even though the liquid product is sticky.

Bonus Round - Cut your fertilizer back, say by 30% to start and gradually pull back to
about 50% of your traditional use. You'll see greener plants, with lest costs and hassle. Cut water as well, adjusting for your environment and see residual benefits in decreased water use.

You should also see- due to your plant's increased uptake- healthier,
larger and higher quantity output, plus disease and fungi free plants
that are resistant to salts in the water and to many pests.

Test, Observe, Increase 

Test your organic matter before you start and chart progress as you go. 

You should notice an increase in organic matter a few months after you start regular use, increased moisture retention in the soil and all the other benefits of the product.
